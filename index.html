The Challenge

WHY THIS SYSTEM IS NEEDED

Underwater environments suffer from low visibility, color distortion, scattering, and noise, which make object detection difficult. This project addresses these challenges by first enhancing each video frame and then performing object detection to support maritime monitoring and security applications.

🌊

Color Attenuation

Red and orange wavelengths are absorbed within meters of the surface, leaving heavy blue-green dominance that confuses detectors.

🌫️

Scattering & Haze

Suspended particles diffuse light and reduce contrast, smearing fine details needed for threat identification.

🔦

Low Illumination

Deep or murky water eliminates ambient light, making it nearly impossible for standard detectors to locate objects reliably.

⚠️

High-Stakes Detection

Missing a naval mine or misidentifying a threat in maritime security is not an option — precision matters at every frame.
